Visit your local Yard House in Cincinnati, Ohio to experience innovative foods from our amazing scratch kitchen. The beer menu at Yard House features a variety of new craft, local, seasonal and imported beers on draft and by the bottle. You can find us between the Great American Ball Park and Paul Brown Stadium. Our restaurant is conveniently located near Smale Riverfront Park, across Carol Anne's Carousel. Visit our Yard House location at 95 East Freedom Way today to experience fusion of globally inspired flavors and favorite American dishes.
